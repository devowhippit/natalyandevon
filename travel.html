Travel

The Catskills are a popular getaway destination. We recommend booking as soon as possible.

The most affordable and convenient options are group AirBnbs in West Kill or a room at the Catskills Season’s Inn (discount code NATALYADEVON0616). There are also some luxurious motels and beautiful campsites nearby.

If you can stay Friday through Sunday, we think you should. There are many outdoor activities to enjoy, fun towns to explore, and delicious food and beer to consume (check out the activities page).
