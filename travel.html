Travel

The heart of the Catskills is only 3 hours from the Big Apple and 2.5 hours from Albany.

There are regular Trailways buses to and from New York City ($60 each way), but we recommend renting a car or carpooling if you want to explore the area. If you can, we encourage you to take a long weekend to enjoy the spectacular hiking and charming local breweries and restaurants.

From New York City

New York City Airports

Newark may be the best if you plan to drive directly to West Kill upon landing. La Guardia and John F. Kennedy have buses, trains, and cabs that can get you into the city if you plan to stay before heading up to West Kill. We recommend comparing all three for affordability.

Newark International AirportLands in New Jersey. Cab or train into the city or drive to West Kill.
La Guardia AirportLands in North Queens. Cab or take bus to to train into city.
John F. Kennedy International AirportLands in South Queens. Cab or train into the city.
