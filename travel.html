– Travel –

Newton Farm is only 3 hours from the Big Apple and 2.5 hours from Albany.

There are regular Trailways buses to and from New York City, but we recommend renting a car or carpooling if you want to explore the area. Try AutoSlash for discounts. If you can, we encourage you to take a long weekend to enjoy the spectacular hiking and charming local breweries and restaurants.

From New York City

New York City Airports

Newark may be the best if you plan to drive directly to West Kill upon landing. LaGuardia and John F. Kennedy have buses, trains, and cabs that can get you into the city if you plan to stay there before heading up to West Kill. We recommend comparing all three for affordability.

Newark International Airport

Lands in New Jersey. Cab or train into the city or drive to West Kill.

LaGuardia Airport

Lands in North Queens. Cab into the city or take the bus to train.

John F. Kennedy International Airport

Lands in South Queens. Cab or train into the city.
